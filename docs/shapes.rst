Methods

``rect(x, y, w, h);``
####################
 Draws a rectangle from (x,y) to (x+w,y+h)

 +--------------------------------------------+
 |Parameters                                  |
 +--------+---------------------------+-------+
 |Variable|Description                |Default|
 +========+===========================+=======+
 |``x``   |x-position of the rectangle|       |
 +--------+---------------------------+-------+
 |``y``   |y-position of the rectangle|       |
 +--------+---------------------------+-------+
 |``w``   |Width of the rectangle     |``10`` |
 +--------+---------------------------+-------+
 |``h``   |Height of the rectangle    |``10`` |
 +--------+---------------------------+-------+
