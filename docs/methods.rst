Methods
=======

``noStroke()``
##############
 Sets alpha of stroke to 00.

``noFill()``
############
 Sets alpha of fill to 00.

``rect(x, y, w, h)``
####################
 Draws a rectangle from (x,y) to (x+w,y+h)

 +-----+---------------------------+
 |           Parameters            |
 +=====+===========================+
 |``x``|x-position of the rectangle|
 +-----+---------------------------+
 |``y``|y-position of the rectangle|
 +-----+---------------------------+
 |``w``|width of the rectangle     |
 +-----+---------------------------+
 |``h``|height of the rectangle    |
 +-----+---------------------------+
